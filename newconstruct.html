New Construction

Our new construction projects are done almost exclusively by us, from drawing the initial plans to installing appliances at the end. Most of our new homes are spec houses that are built to be modern and ready to move into. We do not do custom homes however if a buyer expresses interest in a home before it is finished, we can cater some of the finishing touches to their preferences. Since we do most of our work ourselves, new homes take a long time to build and we are only able to build around 1 new home a year.
